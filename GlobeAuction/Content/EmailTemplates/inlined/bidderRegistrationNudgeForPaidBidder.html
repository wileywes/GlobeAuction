  1. Go to www.theGLOBEAuction.com.
  2. Login with the last name and email you registered with as well as your bidder paddle number above. You can use your desktop or your mobile device.
  3. Click on the BIDS icon on the home page.
  4. In "My Active Bids", you will be able to see your catalog favorites and items you are bidding on (separated into winning and losing).
  5. To bid on an item, browse to it in the catalog or enter the item number at the top of the "My Active Bids" page.
