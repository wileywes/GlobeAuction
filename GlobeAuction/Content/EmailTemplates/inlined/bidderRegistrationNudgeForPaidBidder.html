BIDDING DIRECTIONS:
  1. Go to www.theGLOBEAuction.com.
  2. Log in with the last name and email you registered with as well as your bidder paddle number above. You can use your desktop or your mobile device.
  3. Click on the BIDS icon on the home page.
  4. In "My Active Bids" you will be able to see your catalog favorites and items you are bidding on (separated into winning and losing).
  5. To bid on an item, browse to it in the catalog or enter the item number at the top of the "My Active Bids" page.
  6. If you are outbid on an item, you will receive a text to the phone number you used for registration. It will include a link to increase your bid should you want to.
  7. Some items can have multiple winners. Therefore, only place the number of bids that you want to win.
  8. You will receive a nudge email and text when it is time to pay. Pay from your phone and pick up items at Check Out.

AUCTION PICK UP:
  1. Winnings that include an electronic certificate will be emailed to the email address on file.
  2. If attending the LIVE Auction, pick up all physical items (Silent and Live) on-site before leaving the event.
  3. If participating virtually, physical items must be picked up on Sunday, March 24, between 2:00 pm – 6:00 pm at 2833 Northbrook Drive, Atlanta, GA 30340. For extenuating circumstances, email GLOBEAuctionPickup@gmail.com to coordinate a pickup before the last day of school. Physical items not picked up by this day/time will be forfeited without the possibility of a refund.
