This year we have added the ability to search and browse the catalog ONLINE. You can go to the Catalog page right now and mark your favorite items. This feature will allow you to return to your favorites quickly when the silent auction opens at 6:30 on Saturday. To do this, log in with your paddle number (above) as well as the last name and email address you registered with.

You will enter bids for silent auction items on your mobile device. Below are some details on how this will work as well as a few other notes about the Auction:
  1. Login with the last name and email you registered with as well as your bidder paddle number.
  2. To access the Mobile Bidding site, go to the BIDS icon on the home page once it is available.
  3. In My Active Bids, you will be able to see your catalog favorites and items you are bidding on (separated into winning and losing).
  4. To bid on an item, browse to it in the catalog or enter the item number at the top of the My Active Bids page.
  5. If you are outbid on an item, you will receive a text to the phone number you used for registration. It will include a link to increase your bid should you want to.
  6. Some items are able to have multiple winners. Therefore, only place the number of bids that you want to win.
  7. The Live Auction will take place the old-school way — via your raised paddle. Live winnings will show up in your checkout cart.
  8. Check Out: You will receive a nudge email and text when it is time to pay. Pay from your phone and pick up items at Check Out.
  9. Bypass the Check Out Line: If you only purchased digital items and have nothing to pick up, pay directly from your phone. When done, you are good to go!
  10. Buying Raffle Tickets tonight? Purchase them from the store icon on the website. Your tickets will be added to the fish bowl once you pay.
  11. Trouble shoot, recharge, or borrow a device at our Tech Table.
  12. Please leave your bid paddles in the baskets by the exit as you leave.


Note that you must have purchased your ticket in order to access the catalog favorites and mobile bidding features.
