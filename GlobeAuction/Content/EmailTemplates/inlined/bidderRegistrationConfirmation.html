Bidding Directions beginning May 16 at 7:00 pm and ending May 17 at 7:00 pm:
  1. Go to www.theGLOBEAuction.com. Login with the last name and email you registered with as well as your bidder paddle number above. You can use your desktop or your mobile device.
  2. Click on the BIDS icon on the home page.
  3. In My Active Bids, you will be able to see your catalog favorites and items you are bidding on (separated into winning and losing).
  4. To bid on an item, browse to it in the catalog or enter the item number at the top of the My Active Bids page.
  5. If you are outbid on an item, you will receive a text to the phone number you used for registration. It will include a link to increase your bid should you want to.
  6. Some items can have multiple winners. Therefore, only place the number of bids that you want to win.
  7. You will receive a nudge email and text when it is time to pay. Pay from your phone and pick up items at Check Out.
  8. Email auction@theglobeacademy.net if you are having technical problems – we have a team that will be monitoring that account during Auction hours. FAQs and HOW TO videos are also posted on the Auction’s website. We will also have a Zoom meeting open from 6:00-8:00 pm both nights should you have questions you’d like answered in person. That link will be provided via email the day of the Auction. Happy bidding!
